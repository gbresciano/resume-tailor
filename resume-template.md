## Google Docs Formatting Notes

When pasting into Google Docs:

- **Font:** Inter, Arial, Helvetica, or Calibri
- **Body size:** 10.5–11pt
- **Name:** 18–20pt, bold, ALL CAPS
- **Section titles:** 10.5pt, bold, ALL CAPS, with thin horizontal rule below
- **Company/role line:** bold
- **Bullets:** standard indent, no decorative symbols needed
- **Margins:** 1.5cm all sides (tight but readable)
- **Spacing:** minimal between bullets; slightly more between roles
- **No tables, no text boxes, no multi-column layouts**
- **Export to PDF** to confirm ATS compatibility before submitting
